This post summarizes the README content for **Sol._.T**, a relationship counseling chatbot designed for empathetic, personalized conversations.

## Project Overview

Recent advances in LLMs have enabled conversational AI that can handle emotionally complex topics. Sol._.T targets relationship counseling by providing empathetic guidance and helping users communicate more effectively with partners.

## Key Features

<img src="https://github.com/coqls1229/Sol-T/assets/127406760/1c291849-e59f-4a5b-bdd6-636226b27ddf" width="480" height="400" />

Users describe their situation, and Sol._.T provides tailored advice. If context is missing, it asks follow-up questions to clarify the situation.

<img src="https://github.com/coqls1229/Sol-T/assets/127406760/562c8951-f04b-4e02-a991-325b76e3afc3" width="480" height="400" />

When enough context is provided, the chatbot empathizes with both sides and suggests conflict-resolution strategies or example messages.
