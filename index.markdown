## About

<div class="about-body" markdown="1">

I am an undergraduate in Artificial Intelligence at Kyung Hee University, advised by professor MyeongAh Cho in the <a class="vslab-link" href="https://vslab.khu.ac.kr/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Visual Science Lab</strong></a>. \\
My research focuses on **multi-modal video understanding** and **3D vision**, with a particular emphasis on **weakly-supervised video anomaly detection** and **human motion generative models**. Through my work, I aim to uncover meaningful patterns in complex video data and develop novel 3D visual models. \\
I am passionate about applying AI research to solve real-world problems and creating meaningful impact, while collaborating with others to push the boundaries of video and 3D vision technologies.
